Privacy Policy for ChatGPT Prompt Saver

Last updated: May 15, 2023

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the ChatGPT Prompt Saver browser extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information Collection and Use

The ChatGPT Prompt Saver extension collects and stores the following information:

  • Prompts that you choose to save using the extension

This information is stored locally on your device using the browser's storage API. We do not transmit or store this information on any external servers.

3. Data Storage and Security

All saved prompts are stored locally in your browser's storage. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

4. User Control

You have full control over your saved prompts. You can add, view, and delete prompts at any time using the extension's interface.

5. Third-Party Access

The extension does not share any data with third parties. It operates solely within your browser environment.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by creating an issue on our GitHub repository.
